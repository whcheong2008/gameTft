# Story-Stage Mapping — The Shattered Veil (Unity Build)

> Maps every story beat to specific mission stages across 74 stages (8 regions). Built for Unity with cutscenes, combat dialogue, and environmental storytelling.
>
> Read alongside STORY-DRAFT-V1.md for full character/emotional detail.

---

## Unity Storytelling Tools

| Tool | When to Use | Budget |
|------|-------------|--------|
| **Full cutscene** | Major story moments only (revelation, deaths, ending) | 6-8 total across the game |
| **Dialogue scene** | Pre/post mission character conversations. Camera on characters, no action. | 2-4 per region |
| **Combat dialogue** | Characters speak during fights — callouts, reactions, banter. Text boxes over gameplay. | Every stage can have some |
| **Environmental storytelling** | Background elements, terrain, weather, sky changes. No dialogue needed. | Every stage |
| **Brief card** | 1-3 lines of text on a card between stages. Quick context. | As needed for light stages |
| **Flashback overlay** | Brief image flashes during gameplay or cutscenes. Used for R8 climax. | 1-2 total |

### Stage Types

Not every stage carries heavy story. The rhythm matters:

| Type | Story Weight | Per Region | Description |
|------|-------------|------------|-------------|
| **Story stage** | Heavy — cutscene or extended dialogue scene | 2-3 | Plot advances, characters develop, clues land |
| **Character stage** | Medium — brief dialogue + combat dialogue | 2-3 | Character moments, relationship beats, light world-building |
| **Gameplay stage** | Light — brief card + combat dialogue | 2-3 | Mechanic-focused, environmental storytelling, combat challenge |
| **Boss stage** | Heavy — pre-fight scene + combat events + post-fight scene | 1 | Always narrative + mechanical peak |

## REGION 1 — The Frontier (9 stages: 8 + boss)

**Narrative arc:** Arrival → competence → warmth → first clue
**Tone:** Light, confident, warm. Almost adventure-game energy.
**Locks:** None
**Sovereign:** Unseen. "Things are getting worse" — background detail only.

---

### Stage 1: "First Steps" — STORY STAGE
**Type:** Dialogue scene → Combat → Dialogue scene
**Pre-mission (dialogue scene):**
- The group arrives at the frontier settlement. Quick introductions: Kael (tactical), Lyric (warm), Senna (maternal), Otho (distracted).
- Kael briefs the militia on formation. Stiff, efficient, slightly intimidating.
- Lyric adds: "What he means is — stay behind the big ones, don't wander off, and if something ugly charges you, let one of us deal with it. You're here to learn, not to die." Volunteers laugh.
**Combat:** 2 waves, low-tier. Tutorial basics.
**Combat dialogue:**
- Kael: "Hold formation. Let them come to you."
- Lyric: "Nice hit, Dren. See? You're a natural."
**Post-mission:** Brief card. "The frontier outpost is secured. But the Voidspawn came from closer than expected."

---

### Stage 2: "Border Patrol" — CHARACTER STAGE
**Type:** Brief card → Combat → Dialogue scene
**Pre-mission:** Brief card. "Voidspawn sighted along the northern perimeter. The group deploys to clear them."
**Combat:** 2-3 waves. Introduces between-wave repositioning.
**Combat dialogue:**
- Senna: "Kael, your Echo is pulling left. Focus."
- Lyric: "On your flank! — Got it. We're fine."
**Post-mission (dialogue scene):**
- The militiaman Dren is shaken — first real fight.
- Kael is already reviewing the map. Lyric notices Dren's trembling hands, sits with him. "First one's always the worst. Gets easier."
- Later — Lyric to Kael: "Dren's going to be fine." Kael: "You always know their names." Lyric: "You always know where they should stand. We cover different things."

### Stage 3: "The Crossing" — GAMEPLAY STAGE
**Type:** Brief card → Combat → Brief card

**Combat:** 3 waves. Difficulty uptick — player might lose a unit for the first time.

### Stage 4: "Settling In" — STORY STAGE
**Type:** Dialogue scene (camp) → Combat → Brief card
**Pre-mission (dialogue scene — evening camp):**
- **Senna's attunement scene.** She performs the daily refresh for Kael and Lyric. Routine — she does it while chatting.
- "Kael, your Earth Echo is pulling left again. Are you stressed?" / "No." / "Liar."
- "Lyric, yours is humming. You're in a good mood." / "Always."
- "I know both of you better than you know yourselves. Don't forget that."
- **Establishes:** Senna reads emotional states through attunement. This pays off throughout the game.
**Combat:** 3 waves. Element-biased enemies — first exposure to element matchups.
**Combat dialogue:**
- Senna: "Water Echoes are struggling against these Earth types. Reposition your Fire units forward."
**Post-mission:** Brief card. "The group is settling into a rhythm. Kael leads, Lyric supports, Senna sustains."

### Stage 6: "The Family" — CHARACTER STAGE
**Type:** Dialogue scene → Combat → Brief card
**Pre-mission (dialogue scene):**
- **First minor disagreement.** A frontier family refuses to evacuate despite the Voidspawn threat.
- Lyric: "We should override them. We know better — they'll die out here."
- Kael: "It's their home. Their choice."
- Senna, stepping between them: "Boys. Focus. We have a patrol to run."
- **Important:** Zero consequences. The family is fine. The player files this as a personality quirk.
**Combat:** 3 waves. Standard.
**Combat dialogue:**
- Lyric: "For the record, I still think they should have left."
- Kael: "Noted."
**Post-mission:** Brief card. "The family waves as the group passes on the return. They're fine. This time."

### Stage 7: "Otho's Notes" — STORY STAGE (CLUE #1)
**Type:** Brief card → Combat → Dialogue scene
**Pre-mission:** Brief card. "Voidspawn emergence detected near the Veilborn quarter. The group moves to clear it."
**Combat:** 3 waves. Slightly element-biased.
**Combat dialogue:**
- Otho: "Interesting... the tears opened closest to the Veilborn quarter again."
**Post-mission (dialogue scene — CLUE #1):**
- Otho examines the aftermath with his notebook.
- "The emergence pattern is interesting. Standard explanation: Voidspawn prioritize us because we're their biggest threat. Makes sense."
- Pauses, tapping his pen. "What I can't quite account for is the *precision*. Voidspawn supposedly come from a completely separate dimension. How does a creature from the Void know where the Veilborn are standing in *our* world?"
- Senna: "They sense Veil energy. Everyone knows that."
- Otho: "Yes. Everyone knows that."
- He writes in his notebook. Lets it go.

### BOSS: "The Veil Warden" — BOSS STAGE
**Type:** Dialogue scene → Combat (with events) → Dialogue scene
**Pre-mission (dialogue scene):**
- Brief. The corrupted guardian spirit blocks the path. Senna: "It was benign once. Something twisted it."
- Kael: "We put it down. Standard approach — Lyric, you handle morale. I'll call positions."
**Combat:** Single-phase boss. Telegraphed AoE. Teaches boss fundamentals.
**Combat dialogue:**
- Kael: "Red zone! Reposition away from the center!"
- Lyric (to militia): "Hold steady! We've got this!"
- Senna: "It's drawing on the Veil... I can feel it pulling."
**Post-mission (dialogue scene):**
- Senna: "These corruptions are happening faster than they used to."
- Otho, writing in his notebook: "Yes. They are."
- Kael and Lyric share a comfortable look. The group is working well.
- Lyric claps Dren on the shoulder. "You survived your first boss. Drinks are on me."
- **End on warmth.** The group is a team. The player should feel good.

### Stage 1: "The Road to the Barracks" — STORY STAGE (MIRA JOINS)
**Type:** Cutscene → Combat → Dialogue scene
**Pre-mission (CUTSCENE — Mira's introduction):**
- The group travels toward the Barracks. They pass through a destroyed village.
- Camera pans across wreckage. Smoke. Silence.
- Movement — a girl, 16, crouched behind rubble. Alert, not panicked. Sharp eyes.
- Senna approaches first, hands up. "It's okay. We're Resonants."
- The girl — **Mira** — stands. She's Veilborn. The only survivor. She doesn't cry. She watches.
- Senna: "Come with us." No debate. Mira joins.
**Combat:** 2 waves. Lock: 2 Guardians. Tutorial for archetype locks.
**Combat dialogue:**
- Senna: "Stay behind me, Mira. Watch how the Echoes respond."
- Mira (quiet): "I know how they respond."
**Post-mission (dialogue scene):**
- On the road. Mira rides on Kael's supply pack when she gets tired. He doesn't comment.
- Lyric tries a terrible joke. Doesn't land. Tries another. It lands. First Mira smile.
- Otho starts teaching her Veil theory "to keep her mind occupied." He gives her a notebook.

### Stage 7: "The Archive" — STORY STAGE (CLUE #2 — THE WELLSPRING)
**Type:** Dialogue scene → Combat → Dialogue scene

## REGION 4 — The Shattered Lands (9 stages: 8 + boss)

**Narrative arc:** Veil frays → Sovereign encounter → Otho's discovery → the fracture → Kael vs Lyric → Colossus → Lyric's death → cost → split
**Tone:** Dread → revelation → rupture → grief. The most important region.
**Locks:** None (encounter mechanics instead)
**Sovereign:** Palpable. Sky flickers. The wall on the horizon is close.

---

### Stage 1: "Thin Air" — STORY STAGE (ENTERING THE SHATTERED LANDS)
**Type:** Cutscene → Combat → Brief card
**Pre-mission (CUTSCENE):**
- The group enters the Shattered Lands. The landscape shifts — one moment solid earth, the next a glimpse of the Otherside. Alien. Beautiful. The sky flickers between worlds.
- Wild Echoes drift through, aimless. Voidspawn prowl openly. Both coexist here — Echoes and Voidspawn, side by side, neither caring about the other.
- Senna: "The attunement bonds feel noisy here. Like trying to tune an instrument in a windstorm."
- The eastern horizon is no longer a bruise — it's a wall of slow-moving corruption. The Sovereign.
- Otho stares at it. His notebook is out, but his pen isn't moving.
**Combat:** Standard enemies, but stronger. Veil instability causes visual effects — tears open and close in the background.
**Combat dialogue:**
- Senna: "The bonds are unstable here. I'll hold them, but don't push too hard."
- Kael: "Tighter formation than usual. We don't know what's coming."
**Post-mission:** Brief card. "The Shattered Lands. Where the Veil is so thin, two worlds overlap."

### Stage 2: "The Observatory" — STORY STAGE (OTHO'S RESEARCH)
**Type:** Dialogue scene → Combat (Protect) → Dialogue scene

### Stage 3: "The Sovereign's Shadow" — STORY STAGE (RETREAT MISSION)
**Type:** Dialogue scene → Combat (RETREAT) → Cutscene
**Pre-mission (dialogue scene):**
- The group decides to scout the Sovereign's perimeter. Assess the threat.
- Kael: "Reconnaissance only. We observe, we measure, we leave."
- They don't get to just observe.
**Combat: RETREAT MISSION (unique mechanics):**
- The Sovereign is visible at the top of the battlefield — a massive 4×2 entity. It does not move. It does not attack directly. It simply *exists*.
- Voidspawn spawn in waves. The player fights them.
- **The Sovereign regenerates all enemies.** Every 8s, living enemies heal 15% max HP.

**Type:** Cutscene → Combat (3 phases) → Cutscene (THE SEAL) → Cutscene (ENDING)

- Phase 3 — Kael: "It's shrinking the board! Tighten formation!"
- Phase 3 — Senna: "Healing suppressed — I can't keep everyone alive. Focus damage. End this."
- Phase 3 (10%) — Kael: "Final form! EVERYTHING! NOW!"

**Post-boss (CUTSCENE — THE SEAL):**
- The Sovereign falls. But it begins regenerating. The Veil is still open. They have minutes.
- Otho, Senna, and Mira move to the Nexus point. The sealing ritual begins. Tears shrink. 80% — stalls.
- Otho: "We need more. There's not enough of us."
- **Senna speaks to Kael.** Calm. Certain. She's read his emotional state through the attunement bond.
- "I can burn my connection instead of severing it. One life. Freely given. Even your principles allow that."

- **Otho steps forward.** Catches Kael's eye. Nods. Joins the burn. 93%. 94%. 95%. Stalls.
- The Sovereign begins rising. 95% slides to 94%. 93%. 92%.
- **Mira.** Standing behind Kael. Notebook clutched to her chest. Shaking. She steps forward. Stops. Steps forward. Stops. Hand half-raised. Mouth open. Nothing comes out.
- **FLASHBACK OVERLAY:** Rapid images — the ferryman's charm, the silent sibling, burning villages, Lyric's face ("Find another way"), Voss's voice ("the only thing I did wrong was stop at three"), Senna's scream.
- And Mira. Half-raised hand. Wide eyes. Not saying yes. Not saying no.

- Mira's eyes go wide. Surprise. Their eyes meet. One fraction of a second.
- The seal slams to 100%. The Veil closes. The Sovereign collapses. Every tear seals. The sky closes.
- **Silence.**

**ENDING (CUTSCENE):**
- The world after. Voidspawn stop appearing — everywhere, all at once. Resonants feel their bonds go silent. The Echoes are gone. The world is saved and diminished.
- Kael doesn't speak about what happened. Voss assumes he did what needed to be done. Others assume the Veilborn chose.
- Kael lets them believe whatever they believe.
- **Final scene.** The training yard where Kael first met Lyric. Empty. Quiet. No Echoes hum. No Veil energy.
- Kael sits. He holds three things:
  - **Lyric's training tag.** The one he mourns.
  - **The wooden charm.** The one he carries.
  - **Mira's notebook.** Unfinished. Mid-sentence. The one he owes.
- *"Find another way."*
- He found one. It saved the world. It cost him everything he was.
- Camera slowly pulls back. The yard. The sky — whole, closed, quiet.
- **Black.**

## Combat Dialogue Guidelines

- **Max 3-4 lines per stage.** Not every wave needs dialogue. Key moments only.
- **Character voice matters more than information.** Kael is terse. Lyric is warm. Senna is steady. Mira is sharp. Voss is blunt. Otho is analytical.
- **Post-R4, Lyric's absence is felt.** No one fills his role. The group is tactically functional but emotionally hollow.
- **Mira's combat dialogue grows.** R2: mostly silent. R5: first callout. R6: giving instructions. R7: coordinating attunement. R8: "For everyone." — she was becoming someone.
- **Never interrupt combat flow.** Dialogue appears as text overlays during natural pauses (between waves, after boss phase transitions). No forced camera changes mid-fight.
