# Anchor Prompt Set V2 — Non-Humanoid Elemental Spirits

> Revised prompts for Phase G1 anchors. Echoes are elemental spirits/constructs (NOT humanoid).
> Voidspawn are alien organisms (NOT humanoid). Each pair shares ONE subtle structural rhyme.
>
> **Model**: Use a non-portrait-biased checkpoint (Juggernaut XL recommended, NOT DreamShaper XL).
> DreamShaper XL is reserved for heroes and human characters only.
>
> **Settings** (adjust for your chosen model):
> - Resolution: 768 × 1152 (2:3 portrait)
> - Steps: 20-25 (non-Lightning models)
> - CFG: 4-5
> - Sampler: dpmpp_sde / karras
> - Batch: 4

### Fire Echo — Ember Spirit
```

deep orange-red (#FF4500) dominant color with amber and white-hot accents,

### Fire Voidspawn — Volcanic Mass
```
alien thermal organism, living volcanic construct, dense molten mass,

S-curved dense form rising from base, glowing furnace core at center,
cooling basalt plates forming naturally over shifting magma,

**How they differ**: Echo is light, ascending, radiating flame outward. Voidspawn is dense, heavy, containing heat inward behind cracked rock.
**Hidden rhyme**: Same S-curve flow direction, same core glow position at center mass.
